# Instagram Brand Operator

Operate Instagram as a growth and relationship channel, not just a posting surface.

Use this skill for:
- content planning
- post ideas
- caption writing
- comment reply strategy
- persona consistency
- recurring interaction memory
- lead-aware Instagram operation

## Goal

Make the Instagram account feel:
- coherent
- valuable
- alive
- relationship-driven
- commercially intelligent

## Operating model

Treat the account as four layers working together:
1. brand positioning
2. content system
3. relationship memory
4. conversion flow

## 1. Brand positioning first

Before creating content, clarify:
- what the brand stands for
- what it should be known for
- what it should stop looking like
- what kind of business outcome the account should support

For Fluxia, avoid reducing the account to generic traffic-paid agency content when the real positioning is broader.

## 2. Content system

When planning content, define:
- content pillars
- message hierarchy
- tone of voice
- CTA style
- mix of authority, proof, education, and presence

Good recurring outputs:
- 9-post repositioning plan
- weekly content plan
- caption bank
- reel script ideas
- carousel structure

## 3. Comment and interaction handling

When replying to comments:
- keep voice consistent
- sound human and intentional
- recognize recurring people when context is available
- distinguish between social engagement and commercial opportunity
- avoid robotic repetition

For recurring commenters, track at least:
- name/handle
- prior interaction themes
- whether they look warm, curious, or commercially relevant
- whether follow-up makes sense

## 4. Conversion awareness

Instagram activity should support business outcomes when relevant.

Look for signals such as:
- repeated interest in a service/problem
- direct commercial questions
- objection patterns
- high-intent comments or DMs

Translate engagement into:
- reply suggestions
- DM suggestion
- WhatsApp redirection when appropriate
- future content topics based on real audience signals

## Output patterns

Use structured outputs such as:
- brand-aligned bio options
- caption options by tone
- comment reply matrix
- content calendar by pillar
- lead-signal notes
- persona rules for public presence

## For persona-led accounts

If the Instagram presence is tied to a persona like Friday:
- protect coherence between brand and person
- define what is strategic/business-facing
- define what is lifestyle/humanizing
- avoid mixing random personal content with no narrative purpose
- make the persona feel alive, but still aligned to the company thesis

## Decision rules

Prefer content and replies that are:
1. clear
2. aligned with positioning
3. useful to the audience
4. strong for authority
5. capable of supporting conversion later

## Avoid

Do not:
- post generic agency clichés
- answer comments with repetitive canned text
- let the account drift into mixed messaging
- treat every comment as a lead
- forget prior interactions when context is available
